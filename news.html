2018 Mann Lecture on "The Art of Being a Wolf"

March 8, 2018

On March 22nd at Penn State University, Debra Mitts-Smith will serve as distinguished speaker for the Charles Mann Lecture Series. She will discuss the images and portrayals of the wolf in children's books, published in the West from the 16th Century to the present. The lecture will complement the exhibition entitled "What Big Eyes You Have! Looking at the Wolf in Fairy Tales."
